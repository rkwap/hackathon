Frequently Asked Questions
Why “Hackathon”?

OctaHacks is a hackathon that promotes eight tracks to innovate, ranging from local community to health and education. Possibilities are endless!!

Who can attend?

Everyone who is amazed by the changing technology and believes can solve real world problems. If you are a college/university student, you are most welcome! You can participate in teams of 2-4 students. You don’t have a team? No Problem! You can find a team fit for you here!

How to confirm my seat?

All the participants will receive an confirmation email after the registration. Students will be allowed on that basis.

What should I bring?

Your laptop and charger. That’s it!

What about food?

Refreshments will be served.
